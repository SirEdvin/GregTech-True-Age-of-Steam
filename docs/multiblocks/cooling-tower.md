The Cooling Tower is a second multiblock designed to provide **passive cooling**, basically just upgraded version of [Cooling Box](./cooling-box.md).

## How it works

It works basically the same as [Cooling Box](./cooling-box.md), but it has 30 times more capacity and accumulates cooling 10 times faster. Also, any recipe inside is 10 times faster than in [Cooling Box](./cooling-box.md).

!!! tip
    Right-click the controller to see the current accumulated cooling capacity and the maximum for your installed coil tier.
