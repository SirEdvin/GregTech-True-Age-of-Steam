Industrial gas pressurizer is here to address one of the most problematic issues with steam - you need so much of it to get anything powered at all.

## How it works

Industrial gas pressurizer can help you compress steam into its respective dense variants. While this conversion come with some volume loss, by achieving perfect conditions, you can mitigate this loss. By default, you suppose to lose 25% of original steam volume.

Perfect condition for pressurizer is quite simple, between each craft there should be a 2 seconds cooldown and each input hatch should be filled between 35% to 85% of its volume.

By using infused compressed compression springs, that can be crafted from compression cometal (requires [Concept Infusion Matrix](./concept-infusion-matrix.md) to obtain), you can negate volume loss in any conditions and with perfect conditions even increase yield of produced dense steam.
